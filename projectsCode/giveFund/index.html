So What Exactly is Give Fund?

Give Fund is a proposed Snapchat feature aimed at helping foster youth nonprofits raise funds for their impactful programs. Give Fund would let users donate in two ways: their own funds or Snap Give Coins. Users would earn 1 Give Coin for every sponsored video from nonprofits. Users can keep track of their Give Coins in their user profile or under a nonprofit's campaign progress bar. When a user donates to a nonprofit, their bitmoji gets added to the Bitmoji community to show their support.

It was particularly special as I worked on a cross-functional team, implemented both front and back-end functionalities, and utilized React Native with related libraries all for the first time!
