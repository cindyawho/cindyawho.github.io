Different Types of Images

From Interneting Is Hard

JPG Images

JPG images are good for photos, cannot be transparent.

GIF Images

GIFs are good for animations.

PNG Images

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons, can be transparent or deal with opacity changes.

SVG Images

SVGs are amazing. Use them wherever you can.
